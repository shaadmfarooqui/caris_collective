CARIS SIGNAL

Signal → Insight → Action Operating View

Caris Signal is the leadership theme for this dashboard suite: take scattered activity from existing systems, surface the clearest commercial or educational signal, translate it into an account-level insight, and turn that insight into faster, lower-friction action. This page rebrands the broader dashboard concept under the same visual system as the event dashboards.

What Caris Signal is solving

Use existing systems firstSalesforce, Tableau, event tracking, and campaign activity already contain signal. The goal is to connect what exists rather than force a disruptive rebuild.
Minimal workflow disruptionThe operating model should fit current rep, marketing, and analytics workflows while reducing manual synthesis and follow-up lag.
Better prioritizationNot every attendee, account, or KOL should be treated the same. Caris Signal helps identify where action is most likely to matter.

How the model works
1

Capture signal

Pull visible event, campaign, account, ordering, and CRM activity into one operating view.

2

Translate raw data into account-level priorities, KOL flags, follow-up urgency, and likely nurture paths.

3

Drive action

Trigger targeted rep follow-up, education reinforcement, admin support, and leadership reporting with less manual work.

Phased build under the Caris Signal theme
Phase 1
Unified reporting

Standardize dashboards across speaker programs and campaign views so leadership gets one readout style and teams stop rebuilding the same story manually.

Phase 2
Guided workflows

Layer in recommendations, follow-up prompts, segmentation, and account planning logic so teams know where to act next and turn around faster.

Phase 3
Agentic support

Move into semi-automated and agentic workflows that reduce manual synthesis, speed compliance-friendly follow-up, and improve rep and marketing throughput.

What We’re Building
A lightweight intelligence layer (“Collective”) that measures KOL impact beyond attendance, identifies which speakers, topics, and formats drive engagement, begins building a proprietary attribution model linking events → behavior → outcomes, and delivers actionable recommendations instead of just dashboards. simple response tracking to quantify how speaker events drive account-level behavior, clinical adoption, and downstream revenue impact.

Proposed Attribution Models

  • Time-Decay Conversion Model: Weights impact across 30/60/90 day windows
  • Content Engagement Attribution: Links discussion topics to downstream activity
